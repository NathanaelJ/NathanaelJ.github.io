Extended Project

An investigation into the feasibility of ion propulsion for spacecraft and aircraft.

Alongside A-Level studies, I undertook a research project into electric propulsion, exploring the question 'is ion propulsion the future of space and air travel?'. While ion propulsion is currently used on many unmanned spacecraft, it has not yet been made viable for atmospheric transportation. This is a result of the way in which ion propulsion generates thrust, which is extremely efficient for space transport, although very weak. You can read the detailed report below.
