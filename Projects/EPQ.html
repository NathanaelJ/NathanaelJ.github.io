Extended Project

An investigation into the feasibility of ion propulsion for spacecraft and aircraft.

Alongside A-Level studies, I conducted a research project in electric propulsion, exploring the question, 'is ion propulsion the future of space and air travel?'. While ion propulsion currently powers many unmanned spacecraft, it has not yet been made viable for atmospheric vehicles. This is a result of the way in which ion propulsion generates thrust, which is extremely efficient, but incredibly weak. You can read the detailed report below.
