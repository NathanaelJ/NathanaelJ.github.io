A Beginners Guide to CFD

A simple introduction to computational fluid dynamics for F1inSchools students

This document is designed to introduce young students to computational fluid dynamics, without the use of advanced mathematics. It is particularly targeted at 'F1inSchools' teams. Competitors often present poor-quality CFD simulations as a result of internet resources being overly complex. Using simple mathematics, it is possible to explain, in broad terms, how most CFD programs work, their applications, and limitations. In particular, this document focuses on how to properly select appropriate simulation settings. It is important to note that this is not a fully comprehensive guide, and every software uses a slightly different approach. If readers find an error, or need clarification on any part of this document, they are encouraged to email me.
